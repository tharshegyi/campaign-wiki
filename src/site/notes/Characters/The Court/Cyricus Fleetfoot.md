Cyricus Fleetfoot is a member of [[Sunol/The Royal Court\|The Royal Court]] that rules out of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]], in the [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun#Court District\|Court District]].

The First Magistrate, Cyricus is a [[Sunol/Races#The Small Races\|Halfling]] scholar who is an expert in jurisdiction and helps the king rule fairly, according to the law. He studied extensively at the [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Aetherium Institute\|Aetherium Institute]].

Cyricus wears very fine clothes, He has pale skin and brown eyes. He has short, curly dark brown hair and thick sideburns. He is tall (for a halfling), thin, and quick, but suffers from bad allergies.

He is smart and persuasive, but somewhat oblivious to the world around him. believes it is important to keep things clean and tidy. He believes you should be content with what you have.
