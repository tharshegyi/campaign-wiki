A member of [[Sunol/The Royal Court\|The Royal Court]] that rules out of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]], in the [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun#Court District\|Court District]].

The Royal Spymaster, Gier Goldmail, is the mastermind managing a spy network in order to protect the king from hostile schemes, while carrying out the [[Characters/The Court/King William Ambrose\|King]]'s own shady plots

Gier Goldmail wears pointedly unassuming clothing, and looks like any other [[Sunol/Races#Dwarves\|Dwarven]] man one could find down at a tavern. He has ruddy skin and deep-set brown eyes. He has short, curly red and gray hair. He has a long red and gray beard, styled in elaborate braids.

Gier believes it is important to have money and create. He believes freedom can be sacrificed for safety and security. He believes everyone has secrets.
