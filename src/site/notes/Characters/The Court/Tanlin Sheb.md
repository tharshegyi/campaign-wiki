A member of [[Sunol/The Royal Court\|The Royal Court]] that rules out of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]], in the [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun#Court District\|Court District]].

The Provost Tanlin Sheb acts as the elected mayor of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]], who handles practical business of the main city (disposing of thrash, housing, urban health, security, etc.) so that [[Characters/The Court/King William Ambrose\|The King]] doesnâ€™t have to do it himself while also managing a whole realm. 

Tanlin wears very fine clothes and a practiced smile. He is a [[Sunol/Races#The Small Races\|rock gnome]], and therefore quite small, with dark skin and big, bright hazel eyes. He has medium-length thinning, curly brown and gray hair that shows his age. He likes to jog in place.

He cares deeply for this city, it is where he grew up and studied at [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun Academy\|Eversun Academy]]. He is incredibly well-read, and believes it is important to respect tradition and he appreciates the value of an ordered society. He is a devout follower of [[Beiria/The Gods/The Ten\|The Ten]].
