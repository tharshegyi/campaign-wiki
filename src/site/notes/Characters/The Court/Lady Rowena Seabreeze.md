A member of [[Sunol/The Royal Court\|The Royal Court]] that rules out of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]], in the [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun#Court District\|Court District]].

The Admiral Lady Rowena Seabreeze is Commander of the realm’s navy and assists the Marshall, [[Characters/The Court/Sir Alaric Voss\|Sir Alaric Voss]] in strategy. She has close ties with many of the large shipping companies stationed out of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]].

A sailor at heart, Lady Rowena Seabreeze prefers to personally navigate the coastal routes, fostering trade relations while ensuring ships uphold the realms’s honour. She wears simple but well made clothes, with a heavy and worn traveling cloak. 

The Lady Seabreeze looks rather young for her position. She is half [[Sunol/Races#Eladren\|Eladren]], and has extremely pale skin and hazel almond-shaped eyes. Her long straight copper hair is most often tied in a ponytail or messy bun. She has many tattoos and a few piercings.

She is proud of and confident in herself and lives for excitement and adventure. She believes life isn't fair - and it should be unfair in her favour.
