A member of [[Sunol/The Royal Court\|The Royal Court]] that rules out of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]], in the [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun#Court District\|Court District]].

Stablemaster Kieran Ililen is Commander of the realm’s cavalry and assists the Marshall, [[Characters/The Court/Sir Alaric Voss\|Sir Alaric Voss]] in strategy. He also tends to the [[Characters/The Court/King William Ambrose\|King]]’s personal horses.

Kieran is half [[Sunol/Races#The Small Races\|wood-elf]], making him about a head shorter than most men and women around him, and wears finely detailed armour over a uniform. He has ivory skin and blue almond-shaped eyes. His brown and silver hair is cropped short, and he has a matching goatee.

He is an inspiring leader. He loves to enjoy life and believes it is important to be confident in yourself. He believes it's better for everyone to be equal than for anyone to be in charge.
