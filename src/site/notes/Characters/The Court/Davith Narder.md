A member of [[Sunol/The Royal Court\|The Royal Court]] that rules out of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]], in the [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun#Court District\|Court District]].

Davith Narder is [[Characters/The Court/King William Ambrose\|The Kings]] Steward, and takes care of managing the household for the king. He oversees the servants, he helps arrange the king's daily schedule of audiences and meetings, he helps arrange the logistical aspects when the king chooses to travel outside of the city.

Davith, a [[Sunol/Races#Humans\|Human]] man, is dressed in fine clothes, and is often seen carrying a number of papers, parchments, and scrolls. He has pale skin and hazel eyes, and is average height and in very good shape. The sides of his head are shaved, with a strip of curly light brown hair down the middle.

Davith is very well-liked and popular, and quite perceptive. He believes it is important to be restrained and to behave wisely. He also believes you should look out for number one.
