Dame Seraphina Rooke is a member of [[Sunol/The Royal Court\|The Royal Court]] that rules out of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]], in the [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun#Court District\|Court District]].

Dame Seraphina Rooke is the Master of Coin, and takes care of everything related to royal funds. She frequently travels between [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]] and [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone\|Brightstone]]. She grew up in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]], and studied at the [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun Academy\|Eversun Academy]].

Dame Rooke is skilled diplomat, renowned for her ability to broker peace among feuding clans, earning respect at every gathering. She is, however, considered somewhat brisk and standoffish. 

She has a quintessentially [[Sunol/Races#Eladren\|Eladren]] look, with pale skin and large blue eyes. Her medium-length wavy silver hair is tied in a ponytail. She has a severe look on her face at rest, and stands about a head above other women in the room.

She is an inspiring leader, always ready with a story or historical anecdote. She believes it is important to be curious and adventurous.
