A member of [[Sunol/The Royal Court\|The Royal Court]] that rules out of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]], in the [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun#Court District\|Court District]].

Prince Allon Ambrose is the half-[[Sunol/Races#Eladren\|Eladren]] son of [[Characters/The Court/King William Ambrose\|King William Ambrose]] and the late [[Characters/The Court/Queen Seraphina Ambrose\|Queen Seraphina Ambrose]]. He is also the twin brother of [[Characters/The Court/Princess Alayna Ambrose\|Princess Alayna Ambrose]].

Prince Allon is considered a philosopher Prince. He spends much of his time at [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun Academy\|Eversun Academy]], learning both science and magic, as well as politics and whatever takes his fancy.

The Princes clothing is quite extravagant like his father - he enjoys the finest silks and furs and heavy embroidery. He has light skin and golden almond-shaped eyes. His medium-length wavy copper hair is often tied in a ponytail. He is very tall and thin, and is in his early adulthood. 

He has an innate presence that is undeniable, and is very smart. He has a strong desire to achieve something and wants the world to be a fair place for everyone. He believes in forging one's own path in life and believes it is important to be open-minded.
