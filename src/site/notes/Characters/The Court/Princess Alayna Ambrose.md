A member of [[Sunol/The Royal Court\|The Royal Court]] that rules out of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]], in the [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun#Court District\|Court District]].

Princess Alayna Ambrose is the half-[[Sunol/Races#Eladren\|Eladren]] daughter of [[Characters/The Court/King William Ambrose\|King William Ambrose]] and the late [[Characters/The Court/Queen Seraphina Ambrose\|Queen Seraphina Ambrose]]. She is also the twin sister of [[Characters/The Court/Prince Allon Ambrose\|Prince Allon Ambrose]].

The Princess Alayna has too-pale skin and green almond-shaped eyes. She has a short bob of wavy copper hair. She is fairly tall, given her heritage, and is in her young adulthood. She has a strong desire to achieve something and just does what feels right. She believes it is far better to be kind than to be smart.

The Princess is rarely seen out in public.
