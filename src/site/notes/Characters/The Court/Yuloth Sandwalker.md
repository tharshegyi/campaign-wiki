A member of [[Sunol/The Royal Court\|The Royal Court]] that rules out of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]], in the [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun#Court District\|Court District]].

Yuloth Sandwalker is the Archmage of the crown, an exceptional wizard who helps [[Characters/The Court/King William Ambrose\|The King]] handle problems of magical nature.

Yuloth Sandwalker is a [[Sunol/Races#Tieflings\|Tiefling]] wizard hailing from the [[Sunol/The Red Basin/Red Basin\|Red Basin]]. She studied at the [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Aetherium Institute\|Aetherium Institute]] before coming to [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]] in her adulthood.

Yuloth is generally seen wearing good-quality robes, carrying a number of books and parchments, and has a pair of spectacles hanging from a chain around her neck. She has light purple skin and totally black eyes with no visible sclera. She has short, wavy black hair, and large, segmented horns sweep backwards from her head. She is fairly tall, by human woman standards.

She is brilliantly persuasive, but has a bit of a temper. She takes her responsibilities seriously and believes it is important to be forgiving. She believes the pursuit of pleasure is empty.
