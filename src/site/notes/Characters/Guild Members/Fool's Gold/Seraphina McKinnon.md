Seraphina is an adventurer, part of the [[Guilds/The Foragers\|The Foragers]] adventuring party run out of the [[Guilds/Fool's Gold Adventurers Guild\|Fool's Gold Adventurers Guild]] in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]].

Seraphina is an older [[Sunol/Races#Humans\|human]] lady, who wears high-quality armour and an air of confidence. She has pale skin, blue eyes, and straight salt-and-pepper hair is twisted into many very long, tight braids. She constantly fiddles with a coin. She is quite well built despite her age, and keeps strong and nimble through her training as her parties fighter.

She is polite and well-mannered, and speaks with an air of wisdom. She believes it is important to be mindful of nature and devout, and is a follower of [[Beiria/The Gods/The Crone\|The Crone]]. Seraphina acts as the leader of [[Guilds/The Foragers\|The Foragers]] party, and also as a leader for the [[Guilds/Fool's Gold Adventurers Guild\|Fool's Gold Adventurers Guild]] as an experienced adventurer who takes many younger members under her wing.
