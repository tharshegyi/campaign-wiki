Duergath is an adventurer, part of the [[Guilds/The Foragers\|The Foragers]] adventuring party run out of the [[Guilds/Fool's Gold Adventurers Guild\|Fool's Gold Adventurers Guild]] in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]].

Duergath is an older [[Sunol/Races#Dwarves\|dwarven]] druid, who wears simple hide clothing adorned with feathers. He has deep brown skin, deep-set brown eyes, and is completely bald. However, he has a long silver beard that is always intricately braided.

He is sought-after for his sage advice, especially in matters concerning the land. He believes independence is lonely - its better to be part of a group, and as such intends to remain part of his party and guild for the rest of his life.
