Pelt 'Pel' Moon is an adventurer, part of [[Guilds/The Goldseekers\|The Goldseekers]] adventuring party run out of the [[Guilds/Banner Vale Adventurers Guild\|Banner Vale Adventurers Guild]] in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]].

Pelt Moon, or 'Pel', is a female [[Sunol/Races#Migrants\|tabaxi]] sorcerer, who wears fine clothes with intricate beading that are clearly reminiscent of the [[Beiria/Sukrea/Vaanes Republic\|Vaanes Republic]] in [[Beiria/Sukrea/Sukrea\|Sukrea]]. She has light orange fur with small black spots, and yellow eyes, and is quite short by human standards.

Pel is quite optimistic, and heavily motivated by earning coin. She enjoys gambling and will make a bet on almost anything.
