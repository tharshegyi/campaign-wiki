William is an adventurer, part of the [[Guilds/Dawn's Vanguard\|Dawn's Vanguard]] adventuring party run out of the [[Guilds/Banner Vale Adventurers Guild\|Banner Vale Adventurers Guild]] in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]].

William is a [[Sunol/Races#Humans\|human]] paladin that wears shining armor that almost seems to glow with light. He has pale skin, hazel eyes, and medium-length curly light brown hair with a matching goatee. He is broad-shouldered but still has quick reflexes.

He acts as the face of the [[Guilds/Dawn's Vanguard\|Dawn's Vanguard]], and is incredibly charismatic. He is also rumoured to be incredibly cutthroat.
