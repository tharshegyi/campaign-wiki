Nicholas Darby is an adventurer, part of [[Guilds/The Goldseekers\|The Goldseekers]] adventuring party run out of the [[Guilds/Banner Vale Adventurers Guild\|Banner Vale Adventurers Guild]] in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]].

Nicholas is a [[Sunol/Races#Humans\|human]] ranger, who dresses in well-worn traveling clothes and leather armour. He has pale skin, blue eyes, and medium-length straight dark brown and grey hair. He has a face that's seen a few fights - broken nose, scarred ear.

He is level-headed and even-tempered, and believes it is important to be a productive member of society and be obedient. He believes everyone should mind their own business.
