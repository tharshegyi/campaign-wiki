Raf is an adventurer, part of the [[Guilds/Dawn's Vanguard\|Dawn's Vanguard]] adventuring party run out of the [[Guilds/Banner Vale Adventurers Guild\|Banner Vale Adventurers Guild]] in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]].

Raf is a [[Sunol/Races#Humans\|human]] warlock, who wears fine robes and an air of exotic mystery. He has pale skin, brown eyes, and straight black hair twisted into many short, tight braids. He often fiddles and fidgets nervously.

Raf is incredibly well educated, having studied at the [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Aetherium Institute\|Aetherium Institute]] before joining [[Guilds/Banner Vale Adventurers Guild\|Banner Vale Adventurers Guild]].  He is well respected for his arcane knowledge and abilities.
