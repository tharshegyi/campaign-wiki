Gerard is an adventurer, part of the [[Guilds/Blood Oath\|Blood Oath]] adventuring party run out of the [[Guilds/Banner Vale Adventurers Guild\|Banner Vale Adventurers Guild]] in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]].

He is a [[Sunol/Races#Humans\|human]] sorcerer who wears mystic robes and an air of mystery. He has pale skin, hazel eyes, and dark brown and grey hair cropped short. He is thin and nimble, and tends to spend his time observing his surroundings rather than making eye contact.

He is uncommonly clever, and believes in forging one's own path in life. He acts as the head of the [[Guilds/Blood Oath\|Blood Oath]] adventuring party and is well respected in his guild. He is incredibly charismatic and often speaks on behalf of his entire party. He was educated at [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun Academy\|Eversun Academy]] and is incredibly knowledgable about all things arcane and alchemical.
