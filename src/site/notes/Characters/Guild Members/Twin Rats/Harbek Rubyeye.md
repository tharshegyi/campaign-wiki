Harbek is an adventurer, part of [[Guilds/The Sandguard\|The Sandguard]] adventuring party run out of the [[Guilds/Twin Rats Adventurers Guild\|Twin Rats Adventurers Guild]] in [[Sunol/The Red Basin/Vein's End\|Vein's End]].

Harbek wears mystic robes and often carries a number of books and scrolls. He has light brown skin, deep-set hazel eyes, and medium-length, wavy black hair. He has a long black beard. He is pretty big and tough, for a wizard and for a [[Sunol/Races#Dwarves\|dwarf]].

Harbek is a smart man, and also acts as the treasurer and ledgerkeeper of the [[Guilds/Twin Rats Adventurers Guild\|Twin Rats Adventurers Guild]] on top of being one of its primary adventurers. He is close friends with [[Characters/Guild Members/Twin Rats/Thornick Varn\|Thornick Varn]], the guildmaster, and appreciates his vision for a safer [[Sunol/The Red Basin/Red Basin\|Red Basin]].
