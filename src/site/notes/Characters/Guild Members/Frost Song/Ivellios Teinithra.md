Ivellios is an adventurer, part of the [[Guilds/The Inkwell Society\|The Inkwell Society]] adventuring party run out of the [[Guilds/Frost Song Adventurers Guild\|Frost Song Adventurers Guild]] in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone\|Brightstone]].

Ivellios is a beautiful [[Sunol/Races#Eladren\|Eladren]] man, who wears the finest wizard robes money can buy and carries a single small hand-bound wizard book with metallic inlays. He has copper skin, silver almond-shaped eyes that sparkle like stars., andstraight light brown hair that falls down past his broad shoulders. He is very tall, and quite thin.

Ivellios is the head of [[Guilds/The Inkwell Society\|The Inkwell Society]], the premiere party out of the [[Guilds/Frost Song Adventurers Guild\|Frost Song Adventurers Guild]]. He is arrogant, but incredibly well spoken and has finely trained mannerisms. He graduated as valedictorian of his year from the [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Aetherium Institute\|Aetherium Institute]].
