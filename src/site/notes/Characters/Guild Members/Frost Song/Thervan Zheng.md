Thervan is an adventurer, part of the [[Guilds/The Inkwell Society\|The Inkwell Society]] adventuring party run out of the [[Guilds/Frost Song Adventurers Guild\|Frost Song Adventurers Guild]] in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone\|Brightstone]].

Thervan is a [[Sunol/Races#Other Races\|half-elf]], who wears fine robes lined with furs that are common of the [[Beiria/Sukrea/Hyraniea\|Hyraniea]] region of [[Beiria/Sukrea/Sukrea\|Sukrea]]. He carries a single large wizards book on his person at all times. He has ivory skin, with silver almond-shaped eyes and dark brown hair that he keeps cropped short.

He is a second generation immigrant to [[Sunol/Sunol\|Sunol]], who was sent to the [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Aetherium Institute\|Aetherium Institute]] by his parents where he found his talent for the arcane. He desires fame and notoriety, and believes fun distracts you from the important things in life.
