Rhodesia is an adventurer, part of the [[Guilds/The Inkwell Society\|The Inkwell Society]] adventuring party run out of the [[Guilds/Frost Song Adventurers Guild\|Frost Song Adventurers Guild]] in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone\|Brightstone]].

Rohesia is a [[Sunol/Races#Humans\|human]] wizard, who wears mystic robes and perpetually carries a number of books and scrolls. She has pale skin, green eyes, and wavy dark brown hair tied in a short braid. She is nimble and quick.

She is supremely articulate and well-spoken. She believes it is important to be neat and clean and ambitious. Rohesia graduated as salutatorian of her class at the [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Aetherium Institute\|Aetherium Institute]], the same year as her party leader [[Characters/Guild Members/Frost Song/Ivellios Teinithra\|Ivellios Teinithra]].
