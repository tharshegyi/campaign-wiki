A member of the [[Characters/Skyreach Council/Skyreach Council\|Skyreach Council]], run out of the district of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone#Skyhill\|Skyhill]] in Northeastern [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone\|Brightstone]].

Legend is the founder and owner of ‘[[Entities/Silken Fineries\|Silken Fineries]]’, which deals in fine silks and finer clothing. She commands a large warehouse of workers, primarily those who come in from the [[Sunol/The Red Basin/Red Basin\|Red Basin]] looking for work. Those who do well under her get fair pay and good accomodation. Legend is known for her ability to craft the finest clothing in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone\|Brightstone]], when the mood strikes her.

Legend is generally found wearing exquisite clothes with a large coin purse on her belt. She, as a [[Sunol/Races#Tieflings\|tiefling]] woman, has pale skin and totally silver eyes with no visible sclera. Her very long curly red and gray hair is always tied back in elaborate updos around her big, smooth horns, that come from her forehead and curve up and back. She is quite old, and surprisingly fit.

Legend believes it is important to be confident in yourself and to fill the world with beauty. She believes in holding everyone accountable for their actions.
