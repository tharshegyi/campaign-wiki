A member of the [[Characters/Skyreach Council/Skyreach Council\|Skyreach Council]], run out of the district of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone#Skyhill\|Skyhill]] in Northeastern [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone\|Brightstone]].

Roisin Silverborn is a [[Sunol/Races#Humans\|human]] male, originally from [[Sunol/The Red Basin/Vein's End\|Vein's End]]. He is married to [[Characters/Skyreach Council/Epshi Silverborn\|Epshi Silverborn]] and lives in the finest mansion in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone#Skyhill\|Skyhill]], Northeastern [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone\|Brightstone]]. He runs ‘[[Entities/Silver Plate Gemstones\|Silver Plate Gemstones]]', which deals in gemstones and has a hand in casinos in [[Sunol/The Red Basin/Vein's End\|Vein's End]]. He is the richest person on, and unofficial ‘head’ of, [[Characters/Skyreach Council/Skyreach Council\|Skyreach Council]].

Roisin wears finely embroidered clothing and quite a bit of jewellery. He has dark skin and brown eyes. His dark brown hair is cropped short. He is very big and tall, though definitely past his prime. Some sprinkles of white are starting to shine through his hair, and his beard when he is not clean-shaven.

He is gifted with a quick mind. He believes it is important to be wealthy and he likes when others do what he says. He believes taking risks is usually not worth it.
