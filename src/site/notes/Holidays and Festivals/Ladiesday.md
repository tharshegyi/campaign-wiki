Ladiesday is an additional day of the [[Sunol/Sunol#Calendar of Zyklus\|Calendar of Zyklus]], that falls between Arbora 30 and Mitis 1 (the 4th and 5th months of the year). It occurs on the 122nd day of the year.

Ladiesday is a festival celebrating the middle of spring, marked by a flower festival in the great cities of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]] and [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone\|Brightstone]]. The Ladiesday festivals celebrate spring and beauty, and generally involve the crowning of the 'Lady of the Day' in a beauty contest, dances, bouquets, and feasts. [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone\|Brightstone]] is known to hold a special free performance in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone#University Park\|University Park]] theatre on Ladiesday, where a rotation of performers put on shows for the entire duration of the holiday, and entrance is free to the public to celebrate the beauty and art of the city.

This holiday is of particular import to followers of [[Beiria/The Gods/The Lady\|The Lady]], though is generally celebrated by all in small and large wildflower festivals. Some sects of followers of [[Beiria/The Gods/The Lady\|The Lady]] have been known to celebrate a fertility festival on this day, marked by free and open love between the devout.
