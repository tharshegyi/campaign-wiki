Harvestide, or 'The Harvest Feast' is an additional day of the [[Sunol/Sunol#Calendar of Zyklus\|Calendar of Zyklus]], that falls between Aratas 30 and Multar 1 (the 9th and 10th months of the year). It occurs on the 274th day of the year on a non leap year, or the 275th day on a leap year.

This holiday is traditionally a feast to celebrate the harvest and the abundance of food, but also the time when those wishing to travel leave on their journeys before winter set in.

On the day of Harvestide, a huge feast is prepared, either in the home or as part of a community. Farming communities often host fairs, where games and contests such as eating and strength competitions are put on, along with challenges of skill. It is also a day for charity, where churches and volunteers will distribute food and goods to the poor.
