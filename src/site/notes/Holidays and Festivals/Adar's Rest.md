[[Beiria/The Moons/Adar\|Adar]]'s Rest is an additional day of the [[Sunol/Sunol#Calendar of Zyklus\|Calendar of Zyklus]], that occurs the day after Midsummer, once every three years. It falls on the 214th day of the year.

[[Beiria/The Moons/Adar\|Adar]]'s Rest is a leap year day and event occurring once every three years, that was traditionally  used for fairs, bazaars, musical and theatrical performances, and tournaments of skill and magical ability. It is also a day to make or renew agreements and contracts that last the next 3 years until [[Beiria/The Moons/Adar\|Adar]]'s next rest.

Eversun holds a huge festival on [[Beiria/The Moons/Adar\|Adar]]'s Rest in the amphitheatre of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun#Old Eversun\|Old Eversun]], where mages from the [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun Academy\|Eversun Academy]] come to hold a contest of magical ability and the [[Characters/The Court/King William Ambrose\|King]] himself presides over a tournament of strength. People from outside of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]] often travel to the city for this massive celebration, including a delegation from the [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Aetherium Institute\|Aetherium Institute]] in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone\|Brightstone]] that make the trek each three years to try themselves against the scholars from [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun Academy\|Eversun Academy]].

This day is most important to followers of [[Beiria/The Gods/The Father\|The Father]], but is celebrated by almost everyone, especially those living in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]].
