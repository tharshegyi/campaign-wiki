Owned by [[Characters/Skyreach Council/Roisin Silverborn\|Roisin Silverborn]], the richest person on, and unofficial ‘head’ of, [[Characters/Skyreach Council/Skyreach Council\|Skyreach Council]].  Silver Plate Gemstones is the richest entity in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone\|Brightstone]].

Silver Plate Gemstones is a company which deals in gemstones and has a hand in casinos in [[Sunol/The Red Basin/Vein's End\|Vein's End]]. They employ a large proportion of the population of [[Sunol/The Red Basin/Vein's End\|Vein's End]], to run [[Entities/The Jester's Coin\|The Jester's Coin]] Casino.
