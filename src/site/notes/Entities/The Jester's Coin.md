The Jester's Coin Casino is a Casino run out of [[Sunol/The Red Basin/Vein's End#Jester's Hall\|Jester's Hall]] district in [[Sunol/The Red Basin/Vein's End\|Vein's End]].

Given the lawlessness of [[Sunol/The Red Basin/Vein's End\|Vein's End]], it is the perfect place for the construction of the absolute monolith of abundance and greed that is The Jester's Coin Casino. Known for it's huge winnings and huge losses, this casino dwarfs other buildings in the surrounding city as a mecca of sin.

You can gamble more than just your money at The Jester's Coin, and may [[Beiria/The Gods/The Jester\|The Jester]] give you favour.
