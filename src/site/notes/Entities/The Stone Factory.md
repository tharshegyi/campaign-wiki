Owned by [[Characters/Skyreach Council/Baldric Stonefoot\|Baldric Stonefoot]], one of the members of the [[Characters/Skyreach Council/Skyreach Council\|Skyreach Council]], The Stone Factory is a company that deals in stones and stoneworks, from heavy boulders to finely carved marble, as well as some gemstones.

The company is equally run out of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone\|Brightstone]], where most of the higher ranking managers and merchants live, and [[Sunol/The Red Basin/Vein's End\|Vein's End]], where the miners and simple stoneworkers reside.
