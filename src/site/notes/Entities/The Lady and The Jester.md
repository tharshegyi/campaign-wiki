The Lady and The Jester is one of the most popular taverns in the [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun#Trades District\|Trades District]] of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]].

Located around the corner from the [[Guilds/Fool's Gold Adventurers Guild\|Fool's Gold Adventurers Guild]], this tavern is a popular place for adventurers and for tradespeople looking for a stiff drink after a long day. It has a good reputation as a place to find out whisperings of happenings going on in and around Eversun and the lower income [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun#Spice District\|Spice District]], [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun#Trades District\|Trades District]], and [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun#Fisherman's Docks\|Fisherman's Docks]].
