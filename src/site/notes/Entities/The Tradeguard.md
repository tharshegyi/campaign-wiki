Owned by [[Characters/Skyreach Council/Loris Everguard\|Loris Everguard]], one of the members of the [[Characters/Skyreach Council/Skyreach Council\|Skyreach Council]], The Tradeguard is a company run out of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone\|Brightstone]] that specialises in the movement of goods and people along [[Sunol/Central Sunol/The Trade Way\|The Trade Way]].

The Tradeguard employs a large number of traders, wagoneers, and mercenaries that facilitate movement along [[Sunol/Central Sunol/The Trade Way\|The Trade Way]], either by organising caravans, hiring out guards, or moving goods in secure, insured transports.

Workers for The Tradeguard are reasonably well payed, and live anywhere along [[Sunol/Central Sunol/The Trade Way\|The Trade Way]], though the majority reside in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone\|Brightstone]], where the business owner and headquarters exists.
