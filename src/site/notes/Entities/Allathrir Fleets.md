Owned by [[Characters/Skyreach Council/Farro Allathrir\|Farro Allathrir]], one of the members of the [[Characters/Skyreach Council/Skyreach Council\|Skyreach Council]], Allathrir Fleets is a large company that deals in ships and transports and has a reputation for ruthlessness on the waters.

This buisness is largely run out of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]], though often has buisness in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone\|Brightstone]], as the third generation owner [[Characters/Skyreach Council/Farro Allathrir\|Farro Allathrir]] keeps his home and works as a politician there. 

This company plays a large part in the export of [[Sunol/Sunolusite\|Sunolusite]] to other places such as the continent of [[Beiria/Sukrea/Sukrea\|Sukrea]].
