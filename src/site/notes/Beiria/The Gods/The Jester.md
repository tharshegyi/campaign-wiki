One of [[Beiria/The Gods/The Ten\|The Ten]]
The Jesters's Blessing: 'May the Jester give you favour'

One side of the duo The Jester and [[Beiria/The Gods/The Stranger\|The Stranger]]

Gender: Male 
Element: Air
Symbol: Coin
Animal: Flying snake

God of Luck, Music, Wind, and Wealth
God of fun, ‘plays’ with the wind, favoured by sailors both for favourable winds and luck in bets, revered by musicians for ‘spinning’ a tune and ‘carrying music on the winds’

Other names:
Dwarvish: Bizantotor, Elvish: Glingant
