Ekeon is a continent that exists in the far north of [[Beiria/Beiria\|Beiria]], west of [[Beiria/Sukrea/Sukrea\|Sukrea]] and [[Sunol/Sunol\|Sunol]].

It sits close to it's sister continent [[Beiria/Noros\|Noros]], with Ekeon to the east and [[Beiria/Noros\|Noros]] to the west.

Very little is known about this continents history.
