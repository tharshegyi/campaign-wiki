The Vaanes Republic is a large desert country that borders [[Beiria/Sukrea/Kingdom of Zaradorn\|Kingdom of Zaradorn]] on it's northwestern side. The land is predominantly covered in arid desert, with woodland to the north. The Republic is well known for its distinctive language, vocal style, hair styles, music style, and cuisine, with the Vaanes hairstyles  and clothing featuring intricate beadwork and accessories.

It is currently in a defensive pact with other countries of [[Beiria/Sukrea/Sukrea\|Sukrea]] against the [[Beiria/Sukrea/Kingdom of Zaradorn\|Kingdom of Zaradorn]], to try and slow its expansion into their lands.

The fine embroidery and beadword of Vaanes clothing make it incredibly popular throughout [[Beiria/Sukrea/Sukrea\|Sukrea]], along with also gaining popularity with its fashionable but lightweight pieces in the [[Sunol/The Red Basin/Red Basin\|Red Basin]] of [[Sunol/Sunol\|Sunol]]. It is run by elected Minister Opal Dust, a tabaxi politician.
