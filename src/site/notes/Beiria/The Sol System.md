This solar system made up of 9 planets around a central yellow-orange sun (0.7 solar masses) called Sol.

The first planet is a called [[Beiria/Beiria\|Beiria]], which has liquid water, an earth-like atmosphere, and two moons. These moons are commonly called [[Beiria/The Moons/Naneth\|Naneth]] and [[Beiria/The Moons/Adar\|Adar]], old elvish names for [[Beiria/The Gods/The Mother\|The Mother]] and [[Beiria/The Gods/The Father\|The Father]], respectively.

There are eight other planets in the system, each associated with one of [[Beiria/The Gods/The Ten\|The Ten]]. They commonly called by their old elvish names, listed below in order with their Common name - Associated God/ess:

Híril - [[Beiria/The Gods/The Lady\|The Lady]] - A small planet that appears pink through telescope.
Iar Adaneth - [[Beiria/The Gods/The Crone\|The Crone]] - A small planet, only slightly larger than Híril, that appears a dark through telescope. She has one visible moon.
Gollor - [[Beiria/The Gods/The Smith\|The Smith]] - A medium sized reddish-orange planet that has many pockmarks that can be vaguely seen by telescope.
Ithron - [[The Scholar \|The Scholar ]] - A large planet of blue that has two rings visible as an 'unusual shape' by telescope.
Machaor - [[Beiria/The Gods/The Soldier\|The Soldier]] - A medium dark red sized planet that is difficult to see by telescope.
Liafaron - [[The Healer \|The Healer ]] - A medium sized greenish planet that is difficult to see by telescope.
Glingant - [[The Jester \|The Jester ]] - A small planet that is difficult to see by telescope.
Ethirne - [[Beiria/The Gods/The Stranger\|The Stranger]] - A large planet that is difficult to see by telescope.
