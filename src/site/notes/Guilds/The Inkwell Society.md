The Inkwell Society is the most prominant party out of the [[Guilds/Frost Song Adventurers Guild\|Frost Song Adventurers Guild]] in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone\|Brightstone]].

It is a unique mage heavy party that primarily takes jobs that are expected to require advance decision making and magical knowledge.

It consists of the adventurers:
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Frost Song/Ivellios Teinithra\|Ivellios Teinithra]], Male Elf Wizard
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Frost Song/Blendaeth Tiammanthyllish\|Blendaeth Tiammanthyllish]], Female Dragonborn Paladin
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Frost Song/Thervan Zheng\|Thervan Zheng]], Male Half-Elf Wizard
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Frost Song/Rohesia Harlow\|Rohesia Harlow]], Female Human Wizard
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Frost Song/Pleasure\|Pleasure]], Male Tiefling Sorcerer
