Blood Oath is an adventuring party run out of the [[Guilds/Banner Vale Adventurers Guild\|Banner Vale Adventurers Guild]] in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]].

Blood Oath is a strong adventuring party out of the Banner Vale that specialises in fighting monsters. They are known for their dominance the melee tournaments held in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]] on [[Holidays and Festivals/Moonfest\|Moonfest]].

The head of the guird, [[Characters/Guild Members/Banner Vale/Gerard Winchester\|Gerard Winchester]], is a charismatic man who is known to speak for the three of them in most settings.

It consists of the adventurers:
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Banner Vale/Gerard Winchester\|Gerard Winchester]], Male Human Sorcerer
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Banner Vale/Obscure\|Obscure]], Female Tiefling Rogue
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Banner Vale/Elysande Stratford\|Elysande Stratford]], Female Human Barbarian
