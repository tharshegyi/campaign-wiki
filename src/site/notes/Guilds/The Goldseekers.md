The Goldseekers are an adventuring party run out of the [[Guilds/Banner Vale Adventurers Guild\|Banner Vale Adventurers Guild]] in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]].

This is a newer adventuring party that is making great strides in the Banner Vale. It consists of some well known and respected adventurers, newly matched up with their leader Gaston Banner, the younger brother of [[Characters/Guild Members/Banner Vale/Lord Eric Banner\|Lord Eric Banner]], the guildmaster.

The adventurers in this party include:
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Banner Vale/Sir Gaston Banner\|Sir Gaston Banner]], Male Human Fighter
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Banner Vale/Thorin Broodfist\|Thorin Broodfist]], Male Dwarf Monk
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Banner Vale/Pelt Moon\|Pelt Moon]], Female Tabaxi Sorcerer
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Banner Vale/Nicholas Darby\|Nicholas Darby]], Male Human Ranger
