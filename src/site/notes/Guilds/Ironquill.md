Ironquill is an adventuring party run out of the [[Guilds/Fool's Gold Adventurers Guild\|Fool's Gold Adventurers Guild]] in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]].

Ironquill are a strong party of the [[Guilds/Fool's Gold Adventurers Guild\|Fool's Gold Adventurers Guild]], known for taking on some of the guilds more challenging tasks.

This party consists of the adventurers:
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Fool's Gold/Manfred Ryland\|Manfred Ryland]], Male Human Ranger
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Fool's Gold/Ermina Stansfield\|Ermina Stansfield]], Female Human Sorcerer 
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Fool's Gold/Hubert Thornton\|Hubert Thornton]], Male Human Fighter
