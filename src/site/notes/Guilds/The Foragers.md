The Foragers are an adventuring party run out of the [[Guilds/Fool's Gold Adventurers Guild\|Fool's Gold Adventurers Guild]] in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]].

They are a rather relaxed party that has been together for some time, that take a number of quests throughout [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]] and [[Sunol/Southern Sunol/Southern Sunol\|Southern Sunol]].

The adventurer's for this party include:
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Fool's Gold/Seraphina McKinnon\|Seraphina McKinnon]], Female Human Fighter
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Fool's Gold/Druuk Merton\|Druuk Merton]], Male Half-Orc Ranger
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Fool's Gold/Duergath Stoutale\|Duergath Stoutale]], Male Dwarf Druid
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Fool's Gold/Dac Wobik\|Dac Wobik]], Male Tortle Cleric
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Fool's Gold/Harrison Honeypot\|Harrison Honeypot]], Male Halfling Bard
