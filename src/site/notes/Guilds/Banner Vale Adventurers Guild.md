Banner Vale Adventurers Guild is a guild working out of the [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun#Merchant District\|Merchant's District]] of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]]. It is run by guildmaster [[Characters/Guild Members/Banner Vale/Lord Eric Banner\|Lord Eric Banner]], and widely known as the guild favoured by [[Sunol/The Royal Court\|The Royal Court]] for difficult quests.

Admission to the Banner Vale Adventurer's Guild is strictly by invitation only, and an invitation is generally only extended to those with significant adventuring experience, or those related to high-ranking persons in the guild or court.

A number of notable adventuring parties work out of the Banner Vale Adventurers Guild, including:
- [[Guilds/Dawn's Vanguard\|Dawn's Vanguard]]
- [[Guilds/Blood Oath\|Blood Oath]]
- [[Guilds/The Goldseekers\|The Goldseekers]]
