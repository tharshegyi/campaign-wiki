The Sandguard is the main adventuring party of the [[Guilds/Twin Rats Adventurers Guild\|Twin Rats Adventurers Guild]], run by the guildmaster himself.

As the only consistently working party of the adventurer's guild, The Sandguard finds itself taking missions of any and every type.

The adventurers in this party include:
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Twin Rats/Thornick Varn\|Thornick Varn]], Male Human Fighter
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Twin Rats/Harbek Rubyeye\|Harbek Rubyeye]], Male Dwarf Wizard
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Twin Rats/Sora Trushlatha\|Sora Trushlatha]], Female Dragonborn Bard
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Twin Rats/Rubrae Lowther\|Rubrae Lowther]], Female Half-Elf Cleric
