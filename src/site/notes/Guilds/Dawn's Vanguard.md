Dawn's Vanguard is an adventuring party run out of the [[Guilds/Banner Vale Adventurers Guild\|Banner Vale Adventurers Guild]] in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Eversun\|Eversun]].

They are the premiere adventuring party out of the Guild, and are known to take on special missions on behalf of the [[Sunol/The Royal Court\|Crown]].

It consists of the adventurers:
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Banner Vale/Sir William Weston\|Sir William Weston]], Male Human Paladin
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Banner Vale/Taklinn Foamtankard\|Taklinn Foamtankard]], Male Dwarf Fighter
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Banner Vale/Righteous\|Righteous]], Female Tiefling Bard
- [[Characters/Guild Members/Banner Vale/Raf Camden\|Raf Camden]], Male Human Warlock
