Aetherium Institute is the biggest university on the continent, located in [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Brightstone\|Brightstone]].  The main studies undertaken in the institute are archeology out of the [[Sunol/The Red Basin/Red Basin\|Red Basin]] and [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Ruin Hills\|Ruin Hills]], and magic.

The main schools of magic classes available at the Aetherium Institute are:
 - Abjuration - Protective magic involving wards, barriers, and negation of other magical effects (e.g., Shield, Counterspell, Mage Armor).
 - Enchantment - Magic that influences, controls, or manipulates the minds and behaviors of creatures (e.g., _Charm Person, Sleep, Suggestion_).
 - Illusion - Deceives the senses and minds of others, creating false images or sounds (e.g., _Minor Illusion, Invisibility, Phantasmal Killer_).
 - Transmutation - Alters the physical properties of a person, creature, or object, changing its form or composition (e.g., _Polymorph, Haste, Mage Hand_).

The school has a long history of rich magical learning, and a reputation for producing the strongest war mages and nurturing the smartest magical minds on the continent. There is also a strong focus on culture in the Aetherium Institute, as the school strives to produce graduates with a good appreciation for arts and literature that they can use to produce their magic with the finesse expected from an Institute graduate.
