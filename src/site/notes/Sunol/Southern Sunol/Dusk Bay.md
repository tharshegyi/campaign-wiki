Dusk Bay is a small inlet with a narrow beach on the otherwise nearly impenetrable western coast of [[Sunol/Central Sunol/Central Sunol\|Central]] and [[Sunol/Southern Sunol/Southern Sunol\|Southern Sunol]].

It is known to sailors as the only place where it is possible to make land on the western coast, although it is far from safe. It is frequented by pirates and ships that with to make shore without detection, making it a dangerous place to land unplanned. This secluded beach leads to a narrow winding path up and through [[Sunol/Southern Sunol/The Werewolf's Spine\|The Werewolf's Spine]] mountain range into [[Sunol/Southern Sunol/Southern Sunol\|Southern Sunol]].
