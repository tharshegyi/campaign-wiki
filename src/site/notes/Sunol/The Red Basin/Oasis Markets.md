The Oasis Markets are the central markets in the [[Sunol/The Red Basin/Vein's End#The Scrapyards\|Scrapyards]] of [[Sunol/The Red Basin/Vein's End\|Vein's End]]. A vibrant and bustling market, this is the main centre for trade in all of the [[Sunol/The Red Basin/Red Basin\|Red Basin]].

People from the city of [[Sunol/The Red Basin/Vein's End\|Vein's End]] have semi-permanent stalls set up in the central market square, however about 60% of the space is taken up by a constant rotation of vendors from all over the [[Sunol/The Red Basin/Red Basin\|Red Basin]]. Many merchants of strange and unique wares keep the offerings of the Oasis Markets a constant surprise.

There is a soup kitchen run bi-weekly out of the Oasis Markets by [[Characters/Guild Members/Twin Rats/Rubrae Lowther\|Rubrae Lowther]] of the [[Guilds/Twin Rats Adventurers Guild\|Twin Rats Adventurers Guild]].
